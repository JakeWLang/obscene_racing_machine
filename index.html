About Jake

I am currently working to attain my Master of Public Policy at the University of Chicago's Harris School of Public Policy. Most of my work surrounds data - cleaning, exploration, visualization, and publishing (like here!) - but I'm just as passionate about equitable access to resources for vulnerable communities.

I've lived in Chicago for more than five years where I've realized transit and transportation are my top priorities. Mobility is a catch-all term with its subsidiaries (economic, social, and physical) relying on one another. Knowing that, I want to work to change travel infrastructure, helping make success accessible and dreams achievable.

When I'm not dedicating my time to learning new programming tools or helping my community tackle key social issues, I like to paint, work out, and play with my pup!
